Privacy Policy
This app, "HK Cross Harbour Tolls & Time", is committed to protecting your privacy.

Location Data: The app may request access to your device's location (when in use only) on the Toll Alert screen, solely to calculate the live road distance to a selected tunnel gate. Location data is used in real time and is never stored, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

Advertising: We use Google AdMob to show ads, which may collect device identifiers and usage data. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics: The app collects anonymous usage data to help improve the service. This includes which pages are viewed, which tunnel directions are selected, and whether the toll gate navigation was completed. Data is associated with a randomly generated identifier that is not linked to your name, Apple ID, email, or any personal information. This identifier is stored only on your device and cannot be used to identify you. Usage data is transmitted securely to Google Sheets and is used solely for understanding how the app is used in aggregate. No personal data is collected, sold, or shared with third parties.

Other Data: No personal information, account data, or usage history is collected by this app.


隱私政策
本應用程式「揸車過海易」致力保護您的私隱。

位置資料：本應用程式可能在「收費提示」頁面要求存取您的裝置位置（僅限使用期間），目的是即時計算您與所選隧道收費站之間的行車距離。位置資料僅作即時處理，絕不儲存、傳送或與任何第三方共享。

廣告：我們使用 Google AdMob 顯示廣告，該服務可能會收集裝置識別碼和使用數據。如需更多資訊，請參閱 Google 隱私權政策。

數據分析：本應用程式會收集匿名使用數據，以協助改善服務。內容包括瀏覽的頁面、選擇的隧道方向，以及是否完成收費站導航。數據與隨機產生的識別碼相關聯，該識別碼與您的姓名、Apple ID、電郵或任何個人資訊均無關聯。此識別碼僅儲存於您的裝置上，無法用於識別您的身份。使用數據會安全地傳輸至 Google Sheets，僅用於了解應用程式的整體使用情況。本應用程式不會收集、出售或與第三方分享任何個人資料。

其他資料：本應用程式不會收集任何個人資料、帳戶資料或使用紀錄。
