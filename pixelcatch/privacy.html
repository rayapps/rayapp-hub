Privacy Policy

PixelCatch  ·  Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

PixelCatch is a screen capture and annotation app for macOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app accesses and exactly what we do with it.

Data We Do Not Collect

PixelCatch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No analytics or crash reporting sent to any server
  • No advertising identifiers used
  • No usage data collected
  • No screenshots or images are ever uploaded or transmitted

Screen Recording Permission

PixelCatch requests macOS Screen Recording permission solely to capture your screen. All captured images are processed entirely on your device and are never sent anywhere. You control where each image is saved.

In-App Purchase

The optional Pro upgrade is processed entirely by Apple through the Mac App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

Local Storage

Your preferences (such as default save location, hotkeys, and language) are stored locally on your Mac using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

PixelCatch does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

PixelCatch does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at rayapp.cc/pixelCatch/privacy with a revised date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the PixelCatch GitHub page.
